Kendall Lewis

Hi! My name is Kendall Lewis. I love software development and teaching CS. I've taught with Girls Who Code for three years! Through the program I've met some of the most amazing women and young ladies. I'm continuously impressed with their work and look forward to seeing how they'll change this world. I strongly believe in gender equality and encouraging girls to consider STEM from a younger age - particularly since CS isn't something I discovered for myself until after I'd already started college. I'm a current member of Women Who Code Reno/Tahoe - a group of even more women who are interesting, inspiring, and share my passion for tech!

I'm currently looking for work in the Reno, NV area. I'm interested in web development, software development, and QA/testing. It might seem like quite the variety, but I have experience in each of these areas and have enjoyed each of them in their own way!
